\( -6x^3 \)JAWAPAN \( -18x^2 \)
\( -12x^6 \)JAWAPAN \( -72x^5 \)

1.1.2: Nilai Terbitan Pertama

Soalan (b)

\( y = -3p^5 \) pada \( p = 2 \)

1. Bezakan: \( \frac{dy}{dp} = -15p^4 \)
2. Ganti p=2: \( -15(2^4) = -15(16) \)

AKHIR: -240

Soalan (f)

\( y = 7x^3 \) pada \( x = 2 \)

1. Bezakan: \( \frac{dy}{dx} = 21x^2 \)
2. Ganti x=2: \( 21(2^2) = 21(4) \)

AKHIR: 84

1.1.4: Petua Hasil Darab (uv)

Formula: \( \frac{dy}{dx} = u\frac{dv}{dx} + v\frac{du}{dx} \)

Latihan (b): \( y = (2x-3)(x+4) \)

Let \( u = 2x-3 \implies u' = 2 \)
Let \( v = x+4 \implies v' = 1 \)
\[ \frac{dy}{dx} = (2x-3)(1) + (x+4)(2) \] \[ = 2x - 3 + 2x + 8 \]
JAWAPAN: 4x + 5

1.1.5: Petua Hasil Bahagi (u/v)

Formula: \( \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{v u' - u v'}{v^2} \)

Latihan (a): \( y = \frac{x+3}{x-2} \)

\( u = x+3, u' = 1 \) | \( v = x-2, v' = 1 \)
\[ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{(x-2)(1) - (x+3)(1)}{(x-2)^2} \]
JAWAPAN: \(\frac{-5}{(x-2)^2}\)

1.1.6: Petua Rantai (Chain Rule)

(a) \( y = (x+2)^5 \)

Bawa kuasa ke depan, tolak satu kuasa, bezakan dalam.
\( \frac{dy}{dx} = 5(x+2)^4 \cdot (1) \)
JAWAPAN: \( 5(x+2)^4 \)

(b) \( y = (2x-1)^{-6} \)

\( \frac{dy}{dx} = -6(2x-1)^{-7} \cdot (2) \)
JAWAPAN: \( -12(2x-1)^{-7} \)

1.2.2: Titik Pusingan & Sifat

Soalan (c): \( y = x^2 - 4x + 2 \)

1. Cari \( \frac{dy}{dx} = 2x - 4 \). Samakan 0 → \( x=2 \)
2. Ganti \( x=2 \) ke \( y \): \( (2)^2 - 4(2) + 2 = -2 \). Titik: (2, -2)
3. Terbitan ke-2: \( \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} = 2 \)
4. Oleh sebab \( 2 > 0 \), ia adalah titik MINIMUM.
KESIMPULAN: (2, -2) Titik Minimum
